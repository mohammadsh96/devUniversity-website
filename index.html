Course We Offer

Many of our courses offer a digital badge. A digital badge is a micro-credential,
which is awarded to a participant upon successful completion of a course.
The digital badge holds information in relation to the course, the topics covered,
the skills acquired, the year it was achieved, and the awarding institution.
dev-University's digital badges are referred to as open badges
which allows them to be displayed on your online/e-portfolio
to support continued professional development.
